About Kabsu Student Records

This system is designed to manage student data efficiently within Kabsu. It allows you to add, view, update, and delete student records including their subjects and grades in a seamless way.

The system is developed as a project for educational purposes by Robby & Kayla, designed to reflect modern UI/UX principles and responsive design.
